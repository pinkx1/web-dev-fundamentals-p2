My Hobby: Video Games

I love playing video games. My favorite games include:

  • Dota 2 (Support player, 6500 MMR)
  • Hearthstone (Battlegrounds)
  • AAA Games: The Witcher, Skyrim, Cyberpunk

Why I Love Gaming

Gaming isn't just a hobby for me; it's a way to explore new worlds, take on challenges, and enjoy unique experiences. Some of the reasons I love gaming:

  • Teamwork and Strategy: Supporting my team in Dota 2, where every match requires not just strategic thinking but also significant psychological effort. Communication is key, and sometimes you need to find a unique approach to each teammate to keep the game on track and avoid potential disasters.
  • Immersive Stories: I deeply enjoy story-driven games with rich narratives, like Horizon Zero Dawn, The Witcher, and Skyrim. For me, playing such games is equivalent to watching a captivating series, but with the added depth of being closer to the main character and fully experiencing their emotions and struggles.
  • Achievement Hunting: Completing quests and collecting 100% achievements is a unique and fulfilling aspect of gaming for me. It’s not just a side activity—it’s one of my main goals. Finishing a game to perfection brings me immense satisfaction.
